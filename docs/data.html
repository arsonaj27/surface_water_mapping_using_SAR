Data

  • SAR: Sentinel‑1 GRD (VV/VH). Filters: orbit, speckle, border noise removal.
  • DEM (optional): SRTM or local DEM for terrain context.
  • Ancillary: Study area boundary, shoreline masks.

Workflow

  1. Preprocess SAR (calibration, speckle filtering, terrain correction).
  2. Generate water masks (e.g., Otsu threshold or edge‑Otsu).
  3. Post‑process (morphological filtering, hole filling).
  4. Aggregate to monthly maps and compute water area time series.
  5. Validate using available observations (if any).
